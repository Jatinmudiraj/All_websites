A heritage of flavor from the highlands of Gedeo.

Each cherry is hand-sorted at the washing station to ensure only the most perfectly ripe fruit enters production. This meticulous attention to detail is what gives our Yirgacheffe its legendary clarity and complex tea-like structure.

Grown at an altitude of 2,100 meters above sea level, the cool nights and rich volcanic soil create a slow maturation process, condensing the floral and citrus sugars within the bean.
